Dirty Frank's

347 S 13th St,
Philadelphia, PA 19107

The dirtiest, dive-iest bar in Philly. Martin's college hang. Always a guaranteed good time because of the unique décor and eclectic locals. The bartender never ages and has some great dance moves.

There a lot of small boutiques in Old City all along N. 3rd Street, like: Vagabond, Art in the Age, Pinot Boutique (for the wine lovers), Brave New Worlds (for those interested in comic books), Scout (lots of cool vintage trinkets!)...

If you're looking to check out another side of Philly, there's also South Street. There are a bunch of eclectic shops, cafés, bars, and restaurants on this strip. While you're on South Street, try to check out the Magic Gardens, a folksy mosaic art garden by Philly's very own artist, Isaiah Zagar.

If you're looking to visit art or history museums, make sure to check out the Art Museum area in Fairmount. You can visit the Eastern State Penitentiary, Rodin Museum, or the Philadelphia Museum of Art. Hint: If you visit the Philadelphia Museum of Art, make sure you check out the famous Rocky steps.
